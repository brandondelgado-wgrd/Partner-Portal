Is MDR Right for You or Your Customers?

Ask these critical questions to determine if MDR is the right solution:

  • Who monitors alerts after hours or during vacations?
  • How quickly could ransomware be contained?
  • Who responds if an endpoint, firewall, or user account is compromised?
  • Can your team provide true 24/7 coverage without alert fatigue?

If these questions expose gaps in coverage or response speed, it's time to explore MDR.
